WHO WE ARE

Al Nadhar Advertising LLC has been in market since long. We are one of the UAE leading creators of signage and graphics established in Sharjah. Al Nadhar Advertising LLC Sharjah is one of the leading signage producers, with a mountain of experience in a wide variety of sectors. We specialise from small scale to large-scale nationwide roll-outs and comfortably handle international projects as well. We can help to establish a stunning and eye-catching identity for your business. We are renowned for our professional craftsmanship, quality, creativity, efficiency and reliability. We design, produce and install signs of the highest standard to attract new business and underline your brand.

Our complete sign-making service from conception to completion combines the latest digital technology, impressive high-tech machinery and robust, best manufacturing techniques to create products and systems of impeccable quality.
